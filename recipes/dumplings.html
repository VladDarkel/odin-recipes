Dumplings Recipe

Description

Delicious homemade dumplings filled with a savory mixture of ground meat and vegetables. Perfect as an appetizer or main course!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, water, and salt to form a dough. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix ground pork, chopped cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut into small circles.
  4. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over and seal the edges.
  5. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add dumplings and cook for 5-7 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  6. Remove dumplings with a slotted spoon and serve hot with dipping sauce.
