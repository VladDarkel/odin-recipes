Ukrainian Borscht Recipe

Description

A traditional Ukrainian beet soup packed with vegetables and rich flavors. Perfect for a hearty meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium beets, peeled and grated
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1/2 small cabbage, shredded
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream and fresh dill for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add grated beets and carrots to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add diced potatoes, shredded cabbage, and broth to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low, add the bay leaf, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  7. Ladle the borscht into bowls and garnish with a dollop of sour cream and fresh dill.
